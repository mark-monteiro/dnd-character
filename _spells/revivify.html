--- name: Revivify slug: revivify classes: - Cleric - Paladin school: Conjuration ritual: false level: 3 castingTime: 1 action range: Touch components: V, S, M material: Diamonds worth 300gp, which the spell consumes. duration: Instantaneous concentration: false reference: '272' ---

You touch a creature that has died within the last minute. That creature returns to life with 1 hit point. This spell can't return to life a creature that has died of old age, nor can it restore any missing body parts.
