--- name: Flame Strike slug: flame-strike classes: - Cleric - Paladin - Warlock school: Evocation ritual: false level: 5 castingTime: 1 action range: 60 feet components: V, S, M material: Pinch of sulfur. duration: Instantaneous concentration: false reference: '242' ---

A vertical column of divine fire roars down from the heavens in a location you specify. Each creature in a 10-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder centered on a point within range must make a dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 4d6 fire damage and 4d6 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
