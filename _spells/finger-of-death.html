--- name: Finger of Death slug: finger-of-death classes: - Sorcerer - Warlock - Wizard school: Necromancy ritual: false level: 7 castingTime: 1 action range: 60 feet components: V, S material: duration: Instantaneous concentration: false reference: '241' ---

You send negative energy coursing through a creature that you can see within range, causing it searing pain. The target must make a constitution saving throw. It takes 7d8 + 30 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

A humanoid killed by this spell rises at the start of your next turn as a zombie that is permanently under your command, following your verbal orders to the best of its ability.
