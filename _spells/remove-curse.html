--- name: Remove Curse slug: remove-curse classes: - Cleric - Paladin - Warlock - Wizard school: Abjuration ritual: false level: 3 castingTime: 1 action range: Touch components: V, S material: duration: Instantaneous concentration: false reference: '271' ---

At your touch, all curses affecting one creature or object end. If the object is a cursed magic item, its curse remains, but the spell breaks its owner's attunement to the object so it can be removed or discarded.
